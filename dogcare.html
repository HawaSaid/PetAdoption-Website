HOW TO TAKE CARE OF A DOG:

To prepare you for when you get your dog, here's a list of things you must follow according to animalfoundation to keep your dog healthy.

  1. You need to buy food for your dog to eat
  2. You should always make sure that you're dog is hydrated by giving him some water during the whole day
  3. You should provide your dog a space where he would feel space and comfortable
  4. You need a space where the dog can do his business
  5. You need to that space clean regularly
  6. You will need to bring your dog to the doctor to make sure his healthy

If you want even more details on dog care, foundations like aspca can also help.
