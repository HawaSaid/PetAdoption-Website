HOW TO TAKE CARE OF A CAT:

To prepare you for when you get your cat, here's a list of things you must follow according to daily paws to keep your cat healthy.

  1. You need to buy food for your cat to eat
  2. You shouldn't give your cat to much food
  3. You should always make sure that you're cat is hydrated by giving him some water during the whole day
  4. You should be extremely careful with cats while travelling
  5. You should brush your cat on a daily basis
  6. You should provide your cat with many litter boxes
  7. You should clean the litter boxes as much as you can
  8. You will need to bring your cat to the veterinarian

If you want even more details on cat care, foundations like ICatCare can also help.
